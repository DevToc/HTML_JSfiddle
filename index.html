Our Services

Our Services

Business Rating

Any customers, whether a paying member or not, can leave a review on a business, with the rating from 1 to 5 stars. For 1-star rating, customer are asked to provide a government-issued ID, as they may be entitled to financial compensation later.

Business Info

As a paying member, a customer can request reports on up to five companies per month. The report would show the true entity of a business and a history of previous lawsuits. Customers are well-informed before making a purchase of goods or services.

Class Action Lawsuit

1-star reviews on outrageous actions by various companies are gathered for our internal reviews. When we find the complaints have reach a critical mass, we will appoint a litigation law firm to file a class action lawsuit on behalf of all the 1-star reviewers on a particular business.

Petition

Depending on each situation, sometimes it is more appropriate to ask companies to correct their actions instead of pursuing the Court of Law. We may file a petition on behalf of reviewers. The petition could be addressed to the companies themselves or to various governments or the Congress.

Consulting

We provide consulting services to companies in various aspects, like setting the intention for the business success, tax and accounting services, remedies on how to mend customers relationship, sale/marketing and business plans.

Mediation

We provide low-cost mediation between customers and companies. This is a wonderful opportunities for customers who don't find themselves in a petition, a class action lawsuit but do not have money to pursue in the Court of Law. This is also a wonderful opportunity for companies to save cost and learn from mistakes, if there were any.
